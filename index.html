Frequently Asked Questions

Practical guidance to help you choose the right input for your South African farm.

1. Which fertilizer is best for South African soils?

It depends on your soil test and crop. Our soil-first approach tailors recommendations to your field, climate, and crop—delivering targeted blends that improve yield and soil health over time.

2. Do you offer soil testing or recommendations?

Yes. We partner with local agronomists to provide on-site sampling and crop-specific recommendations based on local soil conditions.

3. Are your fertilizers suitable for smallholder farmers?

Absolutely. We offer a range of pack sizes and distribution through local networks to support smallholders alongside larger farms.

4. Do you supply in bulk for commercial farms?

Yes. We provide bulk deliveries and coordinated scheduling to fit scalable farming operations across South Africa.

5. Are your products environmentally safe?

All products are tested for safety and compliance. We also offer organic and eco-friendly options to reduce environmental footprint.

6. How do I choose the right fertilizer for my crop?

Start with a soil test, select crop-specific blends, and rely on agronomic guidance from our local team to align inputs with field conditions.

7. Do you deliver nationwide in South Africa?

Yes. Our distribution network covers major farming regions nationwide to minimise lead times and ensure timely inputs for all customers.
